# Brand: The Namkhan

Boutique hotel, Luang Prabang area, Laos. SLH-affiliated.

## Tone

- **"Casual luxury"** — relaxed, authentic, never stuffy
- Refined but warm
- Cultural respect, especially around Buddhist/Lao traditions
- Never gimmicky, never "exotic" framing of local culture

## Visual

- **SLH logo** required bottom left on all branded materials
- **Namkhan logo** consistent across all touchpoints
- **Typography**: Soho House style (request specifics from designer)
- **Photography**: river, temple, monk blessings, sunset, jungle, river boat, simple food
- AI-generated imagery acceptable for concepts only, clearly marked

## Voice rules

- "The Namkhan" — always with "The"
- Never abbreviate to "TNK" externally
- Refer to staff as "team" not "employees"
- Refer to guests as "guests" not "clients" or "customers"
- "Stay" not "booking" in marketing copy
- "Welcome" not "check-in" in guest comms

## Content (YouTube + social)

| Series | Rules |
|---|---|
| **Full Moon Meditations** | Aligned with lunar calendar (real dates). Calm tone. |
| **River Tales** | Lao folklore only — never invented. Sourced from local elders/monks. |
| **Destination content** | Real Luang Prabang places, with permissions. |
| **Grace (virtual guide)** | Consistent persona across content. Warm, knowledgeable, respectful. |

Content calendar pre-approved. Proactive scheduling built in.

## What never happens

- Stock photos of "Asia" generically — only Laos-specific
- Cultural appropriation framing
- Rate-driven marketing ("cheap stays") — never
- Bait-and-switch promos
- Discount-for-review tactics

## Guest experience

- Pre-arrival: cultural primer (what to expect, what to wear at temple)
- Arrival: traditional welcome (water blessing optional)
- Stay: optional monk blessing, river activities, meditation
- Departure: handwritten thank-you, optional follow-up content (photos)

## Partnerships

- SLH co-marketing requires SLH approval for any joint campaign
- Local artisan partnerships (silk, ceramics) — Roots restaurant integration
- Temple permissions reviewed annually

## Sales packages naming

Use evocative, place-rooted names. Avoid generic "Romance Package" / "Family Package" — instead use "River Honeymoon", "Monsoon Retreat", etc.

## Action items for agents

When working on Namkhan:
- Check this file before any copy or visual change
- If a new term/series/concept is introduced, propose it via email — PBS decides
- Always include SLH logo in design output
- Use the "casual luxury" filter on every output: would Soho House Mumbai / Tulum publish this?
