📊 How dates are scored
Read-only · in production opens as right-side drawer
✕

The agent picks dates by scoring every day in the next 120 days on four factors. The top-scoring dates above the floor get scraped. Goal: spend the request budget on dates that actually move pricing decisions, not random Tuesdays in shoulder season.

SCORING FORMULA (each date)
total = DOW × 0.25 + events × 0.35 + lead_time × 0.30 + peak_bonus × 0.10
All factors return 0–100. Total capped at 100.
