Accepting a slot turns it into a draft campaign and queues the AI to write the full email.

## Accept one slot
1. On the Director page, click a slot title to open its drawer.
2. Click **Accept**. The slot updates instantly — no page reload.
3. You will see: "Accepted — email writes automatically within ~2-4 min (or open the draft and click Write email now)."

![shot:newsletter-accept-slot.png|arrow:70,30|label:Click Accept in the drawer]

## Accept a date range
1. Set the **From** and **To** dates at the top.
2. Click **Bulk accept range**. Every proposed slot inside the range (matching the active group filter) is accepted.
3. If there is nothing to accept, the page tells you: "No proposed slots between … Adjust the From/To dates above."

:::tip
If bulk accept found nothing and you are not sure why, [Bulk accept did nothing](/university/newsletter/newsletter-bulk-accept-did-nothing) walks through the three usual causes.
:::

## Accept everything
Click **Accept ALL proposed**. It accepts every proposed slot currently listed, regardless of dates.

## When is the email ready?
A background writer runs every 2 minutes and writes up to 3 emails per run. In practice a draft becomes a full email **2–4 minutes** after you accept it (a large batch takes longer — roughly 1 minute per email). Finished drafts appear under **Broadcasts** (`/guest/newsletters/broadcasts`) with a subject line, photos and full text.

:::tip
If you do not want to wait, open the draft and click **Write email** — it writes that one immediately. Full steps: [How do I write the email now instead of waiting?](/university/newsletter/newsletter-write-email-now)
:::

Expected outcome: accepted slots turn into full drafts in Broadcasts within a few minutes, ready for [review](/university/newsletter/newsletter-review-and-approve-draft).
